🩸 Lestat vs Klaus

⚔️ IMMORTAL CONFRONTATION ⚔️

🧛 Lestat de Lioncourt

A charismatic, rebellious vampire with centuries of experience. Lestat thrives on speed, intelligence, and psychological warfare.

🐺 Klaus Mikaelson

The original hybrid. Brutal, ancient, and nearly unstoppable. Klaus combines vampire and werewolf strength with tactical rage.

⚔️ Battle Analysis

Lestat relies on agility and manipulation, while Klaus relies on raw power and hybrid endurance. This is a clash between elegance and brutality.

🏆 Verdict

Klaus holds the advantage in raw strength and durability, but Lestat’s intelligence and unpredictability make this a close battle. Outcome depends on environment and strategy.
