🦇 Character Archive 🦇

Explore the legends, monsters, and antiheroes that haunt the shadows of myth and dark fantasy.

🧛 Dracula

Origin: The immortal Count of Transylvania, inspired by folklore and immortalized by Bram Stoker.

Abilities: Superhuman strength, shapeshifting, immortality, and control over wolves and bats.

Power Rating: ★★★★★ (Lord of the Night)

Famous Quote: "I am... Dracula."

🩸 Lestat de Lioncourt

Origin: A French nobleman transformed into a vampire, Lestat became one of the most charismatic and complex immortals in gothic fiction.

Abilities: Superhuman speed and strength, heightened senses, immortality, and a powerful hypnotic presence.

Power Rating: ★★★★☆ (The Brat Prince)

Famous Quote: "Evil is a point of view."

🐺 Klaus Mikaelson

Origin: The first hybrid, born of both vampire and werewolf blood, feared by enemies and family alike.

Abilities: Original vampire powers, werewolf abilities, near invulnerability, and centuries of combat experience.

Power Rating: ★★★★★ (The Original Hybrid)

Famous Quote: "They call me the great evil, but I'm only a man who wants to be with his family."

⚔️ Geralt of Rivia

Origin: A witcher trained from childhood to hunt monsters, wandering a dangerous world where humans can be worse than beasts.

Abilities: Master swordsman, enhanced reflexes, alchemy, and magical signs.

Power Rating: ★★★★☆ (Master Witcher)

Famous Quote: "Evil is evil. Lesser, greater, middling... it makes no difference."
